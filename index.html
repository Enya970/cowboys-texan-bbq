Our Story

Authentic Texan BBQ, made with Essex hospitality.

Cowboys started with one idea: BBQ should take its time, and the people round the table should never feel rushed.

Every brisket goes into the smoker the night before. Every rib gets the same care whether it's a Tuesday lunch or a Saturday full house.
